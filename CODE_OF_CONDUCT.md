# Code of Conduct

**Languages:** [English](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [Español](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.es.md)

## Our pledge

Simple Template is an open, welcoming community. We want participation to be a friction-free experience for everyone — regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions, reviews, and issue threads.
- Assume good intent when reading someone else's message.
- Give credit; accept credit graciously.
- Focus on the work and the ideas, not the person.
- When you disagree, explain your reasoning and propose an alternative.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or dismissive language directed at other contributors.
- Discriminatory comments or imagery of any kind.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or reviews.
- Sharing someone's private information without explicit consent.

## Standard

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its full, formal code of conduct. Read it for the complete framework — the summary above is a friendly restatement, not a replacement.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: the repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, release notes, and any other venue connected to Simple Template.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the maintainer privately at:

**jesus.cocano@gmail.com**

Reports will be handled confidentially. You will receive an acknowledgement within 72 hours and a resolution outline as soon as the facts are clear.

## Enforcement

The maintainer is responsible for clarifying standards and will take any action deemed appropriate and fair in response to reported behavior. Actions may include a private reminder, a public clarification, a temporary cooldown, or — for repeat or severe cases — removal from the project's spaces.

## Thank you

A healthy community is part of what makes open source worth doing. Thanks for helping keep Simple Template a welcoming place.
